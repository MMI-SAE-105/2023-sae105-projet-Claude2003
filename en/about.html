About us

Behind this project is Claude MALANDA, a first-year student at BUT MMI in Montbéliard.

This website is dedicated to the film Black Panther. Our project is the result of our passion for this iconic Marvel Studios film. We created this site to celebrate and explore the many dimensions of the film in depth.

Our main aim is to provide a space where you can find precise information detailed information about the film by discovering it in different ways with the elements developed in other characters Its director, its book and many others.
